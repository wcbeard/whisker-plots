About Whisker Plots

Whisker Plots is a collection of interactive data visualizations and calculators designed to make complex information accessible and engaging. Whether you're exploring data trends, calculating daily metrics, or learning about health insights, we provide tools that are both informative and easy to use.

All visualizations are built with Vega and Vega-Lite, open-source tools for creating rich, interactive graphics. The calculators provide real-time results with visual feedback to help you understand the numbers.
