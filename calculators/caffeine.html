About Caffeine

The FDA recommends that healthy adults limit caffeine intake to 400 mg per day, roughly equivalent to four 8-ounce cups of brewed coffee.

Caffeine metabolism varies by person based on genetics, age, weight, and tolerance. The half-life of caffeine is typically 2-8 hours, with an average of about 5 hours. Use the slider above to adjust.

One standard cup of drip coffee contains approximately 95 mg of caffeine.

Note: This calculator is for informational purposes only. Consult with a healthcare professional about your caffeine consumption.
